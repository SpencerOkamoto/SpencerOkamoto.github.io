MedECC is a telemedicine web application using Angular and .NET to provide a global network of physicians and specialists to underserved communities. The app began as a patient monitoring device for understaffed hospital during Covid-19, then evolved into a broader healthcare solution.

As the team's User Interface Designer, I work to organize and understand our clients' needs by storyboarding various user experiences based on feedback from India, Zambia, and Mexico.
