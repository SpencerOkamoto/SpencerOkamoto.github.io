The study began in Westmorland, California -- located South of the Salton Sea and North of Brawley. A series of earthquakes hit the area from September 30th through October 03rd.

I investigate the interaction among fault blocks and migration of fault energy on the Southern San Andreas Fault. Using Python Data Visualizations, this study became a starting point in documenting Earthquake Swarms.

Here are some Python scripts detailing how to access, clean, and analyze US Geological Survey data. In my analysis, I distinguish between Earthquake Swarms and Sequences.
