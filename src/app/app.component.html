Hi,

I'm Spencer - a designer, researcher, and ecologist.

My hobbies outside of work include gardening, cooking, fermenting, and hiking. I believe in developing an ecosystem that benefits all ingredients in order to create something truly distinct of its parts. This is my design philosophy. As a researcher and designer, I look at the components and how their interactions among each other creates those distinct characteristics. Like with food and nature, the product is reminiscent of its parts - each changing small aspects of the environment - but properties emerge as their parts interact with the evolving environment.
